Short template
Hi! I know you probably aren't looking to sell right now. But a property like yours in Como Residences came up in a meeting today. I have a family specifically looking in your cluster who wants to buy quietly. If the price was right, would you be open to just hearing an offer? With best regards, Alikhan

✦ Why it works
🎯
Disarms immediately — "I know you aren't looking to sell" removes defensiveness before it builds
🏠
Social proof — "came up in a meeting" signals real demand, not cold prospecting
💬
Low-commitment ask — "just hearing an offer" costs nothing, lowers the barrier to reply
⚡
Short = scannable — reads in under 10 seconds, ideal for cold outreach on mobile
Standard template
Hi, I hope you're doing well. My name is Dmitrii and I'm a broker, specializing in Arabian Ranches. I regularly work with buyers who are looking specifically in your community - and right now demand is genuinely strong, with limited villas available. That's why I'm reaching out: I'd love to know whether selling is something you'd consider if the right offer came along. There's no pressure at all - even if you're not thinking about it today, I'm happy to share a free, no-obligation valuation so you simply know what your villa is worth in the current market. Would that be helpful? I'm always glad to be a resource for owners in the area. Best regards, Dmitrii

🏆
Establishes authority — "specializing in Arabian Ranches" signals hyper-local expertise owners respect
📊
Market context — "demand is genuinely strong, limited villas" creates urgency without being pushy
🎁
Value-first offer — free valuation gives owners a reason to engage even if they're not selling
🤝
"No pressure" x3 — repeated soft framing builds trust with owners who fear aggressive sales tactics
